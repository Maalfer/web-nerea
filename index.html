MYLIDEAS, Company from Spain

Participated in the carving and polychrome painting of the Valerion skull, featured in HBO’s “House of the Dragon.” This piece was exhibited in shopping centers throughout Spain. Worked on various projects for the Smurfs franchise, including creating several villages for children and designing eyewear displays in collaboration with Armani. Additionally, fabricated cars for “Paw Patrol.”

PROASUR, Company from Spain

Contributed to the polychrome decoration for Berty’s Burger restaurant, and created a large-scale model of the Picos de Europa for the Posada de Valdeón center. Also designed scenic elements for the Oman Botanic Garden Museum.

Certified Illustrator

Worked on vectorizing designs for video games at Liquid Games, participated in various freelance projects, served as a colorist for Devil Reign comic books, and painted murals in Avilés as part of a community project.
